📊 Key Insights & What They Mean

🎯 Direct Traffic Opportunity: 63.6% of OWO visitors come directly to the site, showing strong brand awareness. However, only 31% engage, suggesting the homepage or landing experience may need improvement to better capture visitor interest.
🔍 Google is Your Golden Source: Google visitors have the highest engagement rate (52.4%) and stay longest (48 seconds).
📈 Quality vs Quantity Gap: We have good visitor volume but overall engagement is only 35.7%.
⚠️ Underperforming Sources: The two smallest sources (HvilleBlast and BamaBuzz) have very poor engagement rates and extremely short visit times.

🔍 Page Performance Insights

🏠 Homepage Performance:
Our main page gets 64% of all views but has relatively low engagement time (14.5s). This confirms the need to improve the landing experience.
🎯 Hidden Gems:
"Partners" and "Resources" pages have excellent engagement (68s and 66s) despite lower traffic. Consider promoting these more.
📊 Content Strategy:
Pages about specific topics (The Summit) perform well. We should create more focused, topic-specific content.
💡 Optimization Opportunity:
Our best content isn't our most visited. Maybe use internal linking to guide visitors from the homepage to high-engagement pages.
